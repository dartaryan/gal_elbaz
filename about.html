About Gal Elbaz

Hi, my name is Gal Elbaz, and I am passionate about designing and developing verification environments for digital circuits. With a solid foundation in Electrical Engineering from the Technion and extensive experience in RTL design, SystemVerilog, and FPGA development, I am dedicated to delivering high-quality digital solutions.

Throughout my career, I have honed my skills in creating robust testbenches and implementing verification methodologies to ensure the integrity and functionality of complex digital systems. My work involves a continuous commitment to learning and professional development, allowing me to stay at the forefront of technological advancements in the field.

Beyond the technical realm, I am an enthusiastic problem-solver who enjoys the challenge of integrating hardware and software components to create seamless, efficient systems. I believe in the power of innovation and strive to contribute meaningful advancements to the world of digital design.

When I'm not immersed in circuit design, I enjoy exploring the latest tech trends, sharing knowledge with peers, and embarking on new projects that push the boundaries of what's possible.
